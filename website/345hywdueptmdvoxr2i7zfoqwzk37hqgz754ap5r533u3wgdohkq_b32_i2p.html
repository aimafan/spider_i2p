  At Cocolino Inc, we believe in preserving history and ensuring that the voices of the present are not lost to the future. Our form serves a unique purpose: to capture your message for future generations, specifically for archeologists who may one day uncover the digital remnants of our time.
  When you fill out this form, your message becomes a valuable piece of the puzzle for future researchers, allowing them to gain insights into our society, culture, and individual perspectives. It is a way for you to contribute to the collective memory of humanity.
  We want to assure you that we take the responsibility of preserving your messages seriously. Your submissions will be stored locally, safeguarded with utmost care, and will be kept for as long as we can - with a commitment of at least another 50 years. We understand the significance of your contributions and the trust you place in us to safeguard them.
  However, it is important to note that circumstances may arise where we are faced with difficult decisions. In the event that we are forced to choose between making the archive public or losing your messages altogether, we prioritize preserving your valuable insights. Preserving your messages takes precedence over exposing them to the public eye.
  Your privacy and the integrity of your messages are of utmost importance to us. We will never share your information without your explicit consent. Your messages will be treated with the respect they deserve, ensuring that your voice echoes through the corridors of time.
  Thank you for being a part of this meaningful endeavor. By submitting your message, you are contributing to a digital time capsule that will serve as a testament to our shared human experience. Together, we are preserving history for future generations and enabling them to understand and appreciate the diverse perspectives that shaped our world.
  If you have any questions or concerns about the process, please feel free to reach out to us. We are here to assist you and ensure that your messages are carefully preserved and valued.
