What is Shotacon?

From https://www.definitions.net/definition/SHOTACON
Shotacon is a Japanese slang portmanteau of the phrase Shōtarō complex and describes an attraction to young boys. Outside Japan, the term is used less often with this meaning. It refers to a genre of manga and anime wherein pre-pubescent or pubescent male characters are depicted in a suggestive or erotic manner, whether in the obvious role of object of attraction, or the less apparent role of "subject", as in where the young male character is paired with a male, usually in a homoerotic manner, or with a female, in which the general community would call straight shota. It can also apply to postpubescent characters with youthful neotenic features that would make them appear to be younger than they are.
... or in other words cute boys.
