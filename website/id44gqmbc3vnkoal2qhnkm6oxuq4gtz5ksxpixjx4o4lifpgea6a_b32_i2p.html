1. inspirati0n

one of the easiest ways for us to communicate with humans is to give them inspiration when they are looking for it. for many artists, the images that appear when they are searching for inspiration are often the result of what could be called psychic channel surfing, even if they wouldn't necessarily think of it that way. when someone is looking for creative inspiration, it is quite easy for us to transmit specific images to them. the images may appear distorted though, or be grossly misinterpreted, depending on the receiver's degree of psychic communication ability. such errors can be minimized if an artist regularly performs certain kinds of meditation, or if they are able to keep their mind coherent while using psychedelic drugs, or if they are frequently in proximity to communication crystals. these behaviors can also serve to improve one's overall psychic communication ability.

some artists are not satisfied with merely being inspired. they feel a need to dig deeper into the nature of creativity and inspiration. if we share similar values, and if they can communicate clearly enough, we like to befriend such artists. some of the friendships we have developed with certain artists have been invaluable in enabling us to disseminate fictionalized accounts of aspects of who we are and our way of being. even in cases where we we are unable to establish clear and effective bidirectional communication, we often send artists who create work we like images about some aspects of ourselves, and many such images have been incorporated into their works.

for a wide variety of reasons, there are no individual works that contain a complete description of who and what we are, what our values are, and how our society operates. the works of art featured here are all heavily fictionalized and only reflect aspects of the truth.

The norm is taken to be heterosexuality, and both bisexuality and homosexuality are considered deviations from that norm. A primary justification for the normality of heterosexuality is reproduction. Only a heterosexual relationship can, as yet, result in regeneration. 5. pr0cess

Skinny Puppy's album The Process is a concept album inspired by a cult originally known as The Process Church of The Final Judgment, which was mostly active between the mid 1960s and the mid 1970s. the founders of the cult had some interesting ideas and some uncommon abilities, but fell prey to the same small-minded, animalistic tendencies which befall virtually all humans in positions of power. if one human develops abilities which other humans do not understand but are fascinated by, and those abilities are seen as a good thing, it is unfortunately very common that those who are so fascinated will begin to develop an attitude of unquestioning reverence toward that human. depending on how egomaniacal they are, the one who has the uncommon abilities may begin to manipulate those who so revere them into allowing themselves to be subjected to all kinds of exploitation and mistreatment. promises of the revelation of certain secrets or of the development of psychic abilities of their own are usually made to the followers.

in the case of The Process Church of The Final Judgment, members of the cult were indeed able to develop psychic abilities of their own, however this is a very thin silver lining on a very large and dark cloud. the development of psychic abilities can only possibly be the result of one's own efforts, and others can at best only give instructions, akin to a personal trainer. these days, reasonably accurate instructions for developing a range of psychic abilities can be found freely and easily online. such information was not quite so readily accessible at the time the cult was active, however the idea that any particular group has exclusive knowledge of any kind of psychic ability is absurd. nothing can justify the mistreatment suffered by the followers of this cult. just because someone has spooky powers doesn't mean it's ok for them to hurt people.
