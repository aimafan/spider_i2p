Rates are negotiable but for basic one-off jobs a standard payment of 0.1XMR seems reasonable. I would prefer jobs that are not illicit in nature, but please give me your offers if you have any, so I can have a look at them. You can have a look at some of my work here on this website.

You can contact me on pbote. This is a continuation of I2P-Bote, a now defunct project which implemented a high-latency mix network over I2P. Mix networks provide formal anonymity guarantees even against a global passive adversary. This is someone who is able to monitor the entire internet and would otherwise be able to find destinations and monitor traffic by simply watching messages go from hop to hop. Using pbote will ensure our communications stay highly secure albeit at the cost of significant added latency.
