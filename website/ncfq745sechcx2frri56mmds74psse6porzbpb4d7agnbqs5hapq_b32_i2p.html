The ero-like website is actually a platform for creating and sharing reports, similar to tweets, but with the use of digital signatures and structured data. Our platform allows users to easily create reports and share them with others, while ensuring the authenticity and integrity of the data through the use of digital signatures and open standards. As an open source project, we encourage the downloading and sharing of information from our site.
