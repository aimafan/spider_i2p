You can request signing by sending your CSR to r4sas@mail.i2p. Required information:
  • Your eepsite address. It must be registered and published on reg.i2p, stats.i2p or identiguy.i2p
  • Your eepsite b32 address
  • Your contact email/irc/etc (email prefered for response with certification status and sending signed certificate back to you)
  • Your CSR file
Requests with wildcard CN will be rejected for security reasons!
